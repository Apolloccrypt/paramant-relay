Frequently asked questions

What happens to my file after the recipient downloads it?
On the free tier, files are RAM-only — they exist only in server memory and are wiped immediately on download (burn-on-read) or on TTL expiry. On Pro with files above 5 MB, the file is AES-256-GCM encrypted on disk and wiped when TTL expires. Nothing persists after expiry on any tier.
Why is the free tier limited to 5 MB?
RAM-only storage is finite. 5 MB covers every document that matters: contracts, medical referrals, legal filings, sensor payloads, firmware chunks. If you need to send a video, use WeTransfer. If you need zero trace, use Paramant. The limit is a consequence of the architecture, not an arbitrary upsell gate. If you regularly need more, Pro at €12/mo is the honest answer.
Can I self-host Paramant for free?
Yes. The relay is available on GitHub under BUSL-1.1. Community self-hosting is free forever. The license prevents competitors from reselling the relay as a managed service without a commercial license. See the license page for full terms.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
Yes. Request a trial API key at /request-key — no credit card required. The key is active for 30 days with full Pro-tier limits.
What is ParaDrop?
ParaDrop is an AirDrop alternative — encrypted local transfer with no Apple, no cloud, no account required. Share files with nearby devices over your local network. Burn-on-read. Available on Pro and above. See ParaDrop.
Do all plans include a GDPR Data Processing Agreement?
The standard DPA is publicly available at /dpa and applies to all plans. Enterprise customers can negotiate custom DPA terms — adjusted sub-processor lists, dedicated relay clauses, jurisdiction overrides — as part of their contract.
What does "IEC 62443 / NEN 7510 / NIS2 compliance documentation" mean?
We provide architecture documents, security controls mappings, and technical reference materials aligned to these frameworks. This is documentation you can use as input for your own compliance process — not a third-party certification. If you need a certified audit scope, contact us to discuss options.
What happens if Paramant shuts down?
Paramant is published under BUSL-1.1. If the managed service ever closes, every self-hosted relay keeps running indefinitely — operators own their infrastructure. The source code is permanently available on GitHub. No vendor lock-in by design.
My team needs more than 10 users. What are the options?
Team includes 10 users. Additional users are €5/user/mo, up to 25 total. Over 25 users, contact us at enterprise@paramant.app for Enterprise pricing. For regulated industries — healthcare, legal, industrial — Enterprise is almost always the right tier anyway due to the dedicated relay and compliance documentation requirements.
