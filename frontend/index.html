our policy

Everyone has a privacy policy. We have rules you can check.

The ParaRules are the principles we hold ourselves to, in the product and in the code. Not marketing copy: every one is something you can verify yourself.

  1. 01

    We never had it

    Encrypted in your browser, held in RAM, burned on read.

  2. 02

    Your browser talks only to us

    No third-party requests. No fonts, CDNs, analytics or pixels.

  3. 03

    No surveillance

    No tracking cookies, no fingerprinting, no ad tech.

  4. 04

    You own your keys

    Generated on your device, never sent. We see only the public half.

  5. 05

    EU soil, EU law

    Hosted in Germany. No US CLOUD Act reach over your data.

  6. 06

    Quantum-ready by default

    FIPS 203/204 today, not “soon”.

  7. 07

    Honest by design

    No dark patterns. Real errors, never tidy lies.

  8. 08

    Verifiable, not trust-me

    Open-core, a public CT log, a relay that proves itself.

  9. 09

    Only what’s required

    An email, nothing more. Data we don’t hold can’t leak.
