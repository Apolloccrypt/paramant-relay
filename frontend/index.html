One dashboard. Every tool.

Paramant is account-bound. You work from a single dashboard; the people you send to need nothing at all.

  1. Sign in

    Your API key plus a TOTP code. No password to phish, no shared secret in your inbox.

    Send, share, drop, and sign — all in one place, every transfer bound to your account and your audit log.

    They open your link or verify your .psign signature with just the valid info you sent them. Zero-knowledge for them, full audit trail for you.

How it works

Four steps. Nothing stored.

Client-side encryption, ciphertext-only transit, RAM-only storage, burn after one read.

  1. Encrypt client-side

    Your file is encrypted in the browser before it leaves your device. The relay never sees plaintext.

  2. Transit via Ghost Pipe

    Ciphertext moves through the relay and is hashed into the CT Merkle log.

  3. One-time download

    The recipient decrypts locally. On download the blob is overwritten in RAM.

  4. Cryptographic proof

    The Merkle root updates. The transfer is provable without storing what was transferred.

Under the hood

Post-quantum, hybrid, audited.

Default core stack is ML-KEM-768 (FIPS 203) and ML-DSA-65 (FIPS 204), with ECDH P-256 hybrid. Extended algorithm sets are opt-in (ADR R006).
