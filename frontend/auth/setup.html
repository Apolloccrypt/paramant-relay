Choose how you sign in.

Pick one. You can add the other later. This is the only real choice in setup.

Use a passkey · Recommended

A passkey is like a key only your device has. You unlock it with your fingerprint, face, or device PIN. Nothing to remember, nothing to type. Best if you mostly use one phone or laptop.

Use an authenticator app

An app on your phone shows a fresh 6-digit code every 30 seconds. You type that code to sign in. Good if you switch between many devices.

Which apps work?

Paramant uses the stronger SHA-256 standard, so a few popular apps won't work. These do: Raivo or 2FAS (iPhone), Aegis (Android), 1Password, Bitwarden, Authy, or Ente Auth. Google Authenticator and Microsoft Authenticator do not work yet, because they only support the older SHA-1 standard. If you have one of those, pick any app above instead. Why we use SHA-256.
