ParaID  Beta

Your ParaID wallet

Hold a credential on your device and prove claims like “18 or older?” with a yes or no. Or ask someone else to prove a claim to you. The data behind the answer never leaves the device that holds it.

Sign in to use ParaID

ParaID is tied to your Paramant account.

Sign in   Try the public demo

1. Your device key and credential

A holder key is generated on this device. Then you fetch a demo credential from the registered Paramant Demo Authority. In production this credential comes from your passport (NFC) at a trusted issuer.

...
...

2. Answer a request

Paste a request link a verifier sent you (or open their link and it lands here). Your device builds a proof that reveals only the answer.

Only the answer, the public key binding and a signature over the verifier’s nonce travel. Your name, birthdate, nationality and document number stay on this device.

1. Ask a question

Build a request for a claim, with a fresh nonce so the answer cannot be an old one. Share the link with the person who must prove it.

Send this link to the holder. They answer on their own device.

2. Verify the answer

Paste the answer link you got back. It is checked against the public issuer registry and revocation list, offline for the crypto and one request for the registry.

Beta: the holder key lives in this browser for now; passkey hardware binding and passport (NFC) issuance are the next steps. See how it works.
