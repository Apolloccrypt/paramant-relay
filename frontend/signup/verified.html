Email verified

One more step. Check your inbox for a second email.

Your email is confirmed and your Paramant account is created. To finish, you still need to connect an authenticator app — Paramant doesn't use passwords.

2
Open the second email we just sent you

Look for an email with the subject:

Complete your Paramant account setup

Open it and click the setup link. You'll see a QR code. Scan it with an app that supports SHA-256 TOTP: Raivo (iOS, open source), Aegis (Android, open source), 1Password, Bitwarden, Authy, Ente Auth, or 2FAS. Paramant uses SHA-256 — Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, and iCloud Keychain do NOT work because they only support SHA-1. The 6-digit login codes come from your chosen app, not from email.

Frequently asked

I don't see the second email

Give it a minute — delivery can take up to 60 seconds. Check your spam / junk folder. The sender is noreply@paramant.app. Still nothing? Request a new setup link using the same email address.

Where's the 6-digit code in the email?

It isn't in the email. Paramant uses SHA-256 TOTP. Codes are generated by a compatible authenticator app on your phone after you scan the QR code in the setup email. Recommended: Raivo (iOS) or Aegis (Android) — both open source. Also works: 1Password, Bitwarden, Authy, Ente Auth, 2FAS. Do not use Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or iCloud Keychain — they only support SHA-1 and will generate codes the server rejects. Full list & why SHA-256.

I already completed the setup

Then you're done. Sign in with your email and the 6-digit code from your authenticator app.
